The Good News

What Is Salvation?

[PLACEHOLDER — Salvation is God's gift of new life, offered freely to anyone who places their trust in Jesus Christ. It isn't something we earn by being good enough; it's something we receive by faith, because Jesus already paid the price for us on the cross.]

[PLACEHOLDER — Every one of us has fallen short and been separated from God by sin. But God, in His great love, sent His Son Jesus to live a perfect life, die in our place, and rise again — defeating sin and death forever so that we could be reconciled to Him.]

[PLACEHOLDER — Salvation begins the moment you turn from your old way of living and believe in your heart that Jesus is Lord. From there, it becomes a lifelong journey of walking with Him, growing in His Word, and living in the freedom He gives.]

God's Word

What the Bible Says

"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." Romans 10:9
"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." John 3:16
"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God — not by works, so that no one can boast." Ephesians 2:8-9
