A Place To Belong

Welcome to Our Family

Wherever you are on your journey, there's a seat for you here. Church Of The Living God is a small, close-knit congregation in Charlottesville built on genuine relationships, heartfelt worship, and the Word of God. We'd love nothing more than to meet you in person.

Come as you are — you'll find open arms, warm faces, and a community that truly cares.

Our Mission

What We're About

Worship. Community. Outreach.

Worship

We gather to lift our voices in genuine, Spirit-led praise. Every service is an invitation to encounter God together.

Community

We do life together — sharing meals, praying for one another, and walking through both joys and struggles side by side.

Outreach

We believe faith is meant to be shared. Our hands and hearts reach into Charlottesville to serve our neighbors in love.
