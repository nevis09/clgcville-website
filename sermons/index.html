Beyond Sunday

Take What You've Heard and Live It Out

A message is meant to move you past the pew. If something you heard stirred a passion for worship, a heart for the next generation, or a nudge to serve — here's where our church family puts that faith into action.

Men's Ministry

A brotherhood of men growing in faith, accountability, and service. We meet regularly for fellowship, Bible study, and encouragement.

Learn More

Women's Ministry

A sisterhood built on prayer, Scripture, and genuine friendship. We gather to encourage one another through every season of life.

Learn More

Youth Ministry

A place for teens to encounter God, build lasting friendships, and grow in their faith through games, worship, and honest conversation.

Children's Ministry

A safe, joyful environment where kids learn Bible stories, sing, and discover God's love through age-appropriate lessons and activities.
