Milestones In Our Story

Founded

A Small Gathering of Faith

[HISTORY PLACEHOLDER — Church Of The Living God began with a handful of believers gathering in a living room, united by prayer and a shared conviction to build a church rooted in the Word and genuine community.]

Early Growth

Finding Our First Home

[HISTORY PLACEHOLDER — As word spread and the congregation grew, the church moved into its first dedicated worship space in Charlottesville, allowing ministries and Sunday services to expand for the growing family of believers.]

Move to Berkmar Drive

A Permanent Place to Call Home

[HISTORY PLACEHOLDER — Church Of The Living God found its lasting home at 2130 Berkmar Drive, a space that has since become a sanctuary of worship, fellowship, and outreach for the congregation and the wider community.]

Expanding Ministries

Growing to Serve More

[HISTORY PLACEHOLDER — New ministries took shape to meet the needs of a growing congregation and community — youth programs, community outreach, Bible studies, and pastoral care all flourished during this season.]

Today

A Living, Growing Family

[HISTORY PLACEHOLDER — Today, Church Of The Living God continues to grow as a vibrant, multi-generational congregation devoted to worship, prayer, and service — carrying forward the same mission that started it all.]
