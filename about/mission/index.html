Our Mission Statement

"Rescue the Perishing, Care for the Dying"

This calling shapes every ministry, every service, and every outstretched hand at Church Of The Living God. We exist to reach people who feel lost or forgotten and to walk with them toward hope, healing, and new life in Christ.

Our Vision

Where We're Headed

[VISION STATEMENT PLACEHOLDER — We envision a Charlottesville where no one feels perishing or forgotten, where the love of Christ is made tangible through a church family that shows up, prays hard, and serves without hesitation. We are building a community that reaches beyond our walls into homes, hospitals, and hard places.]

[VISION STATEMENT PLACEHOLDER, PART 2 — As we grow, our vision is to raise up disciples who carry this same mission into their own families, workplaces, and neighborhoods, multiplying the reach of the Gospel far beyond what our congregation alone could accomplish.]

What We Stand On

Our Core Values

  • Faith

    We anchor everything we do in unwavering trust in God's Word and His promises.

  • Prayer

    We believe prayer changes things, and we make it the foundation of our life together.

  • Community

    We do life together, carrying one another's burdens and celebrating one another's joys.

  • Service

    We roll up our sleeves for our neighbors, believing love is meant to be shown, not just spoken.

  • Integrity

    We strive to live and lead with honesty, humility, and consistency in every season.

  • Generosity

    We give freely of our time, resources, and hearts, trusting God to multiply what we sow.
