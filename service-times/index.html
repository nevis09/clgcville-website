What to Expect

[PLACEHOLDER — Walking through our doors for the first time, you'll be greeted with a warm smile and a genuine welcome — no need to know where to sit or what to do, a greeter will point you in the right direction.]

Before You Arrive

Good to Know

Dress Code

[PLACEHOLDER — Come as you are. Whether you're in your Sunday best or casual jeans, you'll fit right in. We care far more about you being here than what you're wearing.]

Parking

[PLACEHOLDER — Free parking is available on-site directly in front of the building, with additional spaces along the side lot. Greeters will be happy to help point you in the right direction.]

Children's Services

[PLACEHOLDER — Our kids' ministry team cares for children during the service, with age-appropriate lessons, songs, and activities in a safe, supervised environment. Ask a greeter for directions to check-in.]
